# Dog Behavior and Training Overview

Dogs are social animals that thrive on routine and human interaction.
Daily walks and exercise are necessary for their physical and mental health.

Positive reinforcement is the most effective training method.
Rewarding good behavior with treats or praise helps dogs learn commands quickly.

Dogs communicate through posture and vocalization.
A wagging tail often signals excitement, while growling can indicate discomfort.

Grooming, dental care, and regular vet visits help maintain long-term health.
