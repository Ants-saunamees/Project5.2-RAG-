e# Basic Cat Care Guidelines

Cats are independent animals, but they still need consistent daily care.
A healthy cat requires fresh water, scheduled feeding, and a clean litter box.

Most cats enjoy warm resting spots, soft blankets, and quiet environments.
Indoor cats generally live longer and face fewer risks than outdoor cats.

Regular vet checkups, vaccinations, and parasite prevention are essential.
Playtime with toys and scratching posts keeps them mentally and physically active.
